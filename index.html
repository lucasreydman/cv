SHARPRFI

MLB first-inning betting research covering both sides of the NRFI and YRFI market behind one toggle that flips probabilities, break-even odds, and methodology between views. One engine estimates expected first-inning runs with a Poisson model over stabilized pitcher and lineup stats, park factors, and live weather. A second replays the first inning 10,000 times per game with a batter-level Monte Carlo simulation. The headline number blends both, and a bet-value calculator turns posted odds into expected value.
