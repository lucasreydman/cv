CSCI 2691 - Introductory Project

Jan 2025 - Apr 2025

Tic Tec Toe is an innovative social media platform specifically designed for researchers. It seeks to revolutionize how scholars discover, consume, and engage with academic content by addressing several critical challenges in the research community. Researchers today face information overload due to the vast number of publications across various disciplines. Time constraints make it difficult for them to read and analyze every relevant paper, especially when juggling responsibilities like experiments, grant writing, and teaching. Research papers are scattered across multiple databases and repositories, leading to a fragmented discovery process. Existing platforms also lack robust features for meaningful collaboration and engagement among peers. Tic Tec Toe aims to solve these issues by providing a centralized hub where researchers can access personalized feeds of relevant papers, interact with peers through likes, comments, and shares, and utilize unique features like audio playback of papers for on-the-go learning. The platform's ultimate goal is to foster collaboration, accelerate knowledge dissemination, and become the go-to platform for researchers to stay updated and make significant contributions to their fields.
